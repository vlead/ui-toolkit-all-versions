Canvas

The Canvas displays the geometry loaded by the application. It allows to view the geometry and their relative coordinate systems from different angles. It also supports navigation through the scene and modiÔ¨Åcation of the vertices.

Navigation

  • Pan through the scene by dragging the mouse with the middle button pressed or with left mouse button pressed. (see Display tab options).
  • Scroll the mouse wheel to zoom in or out of the scene.
  • Rotate the view by dragging the mouse with the right button pressed.
  • Disable Lock vertices option in Display tab to move selected vertices with left mouse button.
